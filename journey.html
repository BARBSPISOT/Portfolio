Our Journey

PLDT, INC — Company Visit

During our visit to PLDT, I learned about the importance of telecommunications in modern society. We explored how communication technology plays a crucial role in shaping the future of industries. PLDT also shared their strategies for digital transformation and innovation in telecom infrastructure, which deepened my understanding of network solutions and service delivery in the digital era.

JAIRSOFT, INC. — Software Solutions & Consulting

At Jairsoft, we learned about software solutions tailored to meet specific client needs. We were introduced to the process of customizing software for different industries and understanding the balance between scalability and flexibility. I gained insights into the vital role of consulting services in helping businesses streamline their operations through effective software solutions.

VITRO, INC. — Data Center & Digital Transformation

Visiting Vitro gave me a deeper understanding of how data centers are the backbone of digital transformation. We learned about the critical role data storage, security, and cloud services play in modern businesses. Vitro’s innovative solutions and their impact on business operations taught me about the integration of technology in optimizing business processes and enhancing efficiency.

Davao City Central Communications and Emergency Response Center (Central 911)

At the Central 911 center, I learned how technology is utilized to ensure efficient emergency responses. The center uses cutting-edge communication systems and real-time data management to provide timely and coordinated responses during emergencies. This visit emphasized the critical role of IT in public safety and how effective communication technology can save lives and resources.
