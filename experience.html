Our Tour Experience

During the Davao IT tour, I had the opportunity to visit several leading companies in the tech industry. At PLDT, Inc. — Company Visit, I gained valuable insights into the telecommunications industry, learning about the latest advancements in communication technology and the role of IT in modernizing infrastructure. At JAIRSOFT, Inc. — Software Solutions & Consulting, I learned how software solutions are tailored to meet client needs, enhancing my understanding of the importance of customizable and scalable software development in business operations. VITRO, Inc. — Data Center & Digital Transformation introduced me to the world of data centers and digital transformation, where I observed how companies are leveraging IT to innovate and optimize their operations through cloud solutions and secure data management. Lastly, at the Davao City Central Communications and Emergency Response Center (Central 911), I witnessed how technology plays a crucial role in ensuring efficient and timely emergency response, showcasing the impact of IT in public service and safety. This tour not only broadened my technical knowledge but also gave me a deeper understanding of how technology is shaping various industries and driving innovation across the region.
